About Qinyue Global

Your Reliable China-Based OEM Partner | 15+ Years Experience | ISO 13485 Certified | CE/FDA Compliant

Qinyue Global is a professional sourcing and solution partner specializing in hot & cold therapy products. We work closely with reliable manufacturing partners to support global brands, distributors, healthcare providers, and e-commerce businesses with compliant and market-ready recovery solutions.

More than just supplying products, we act as an extension of our clients’ sourcing teams. Our services include:

  • Product sourcing & OEM/ODM customization
  • Strict quality control coordination
  • Low MOQ flexibility (starting from 500pcs)
  • Fast response within 24 hours
  • Full export logistics support
  • Free design & sampling assistance

Our hot & cold therapy products are manufactured by long-term strategic partner factories with over 15 years of experience in thermal therapy products. These facilities operate under ISO 13485 quality management systems and comply with CE and FDA standards, using medical-grade and eco-friendly materials with full traceability.

Advanced automated gel-filling lines and controlled cleanroom environments ensure stable quality. Each product is strictly tested for leakage resistance, durability, and performance. We fully support OEM and private label customization, including size, gel formula, fabric, color, logo printing, and packaging.
